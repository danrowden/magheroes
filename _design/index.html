Luke Tonge

Art director, Boat Magazine

Episode 1  •  25th November, 2013

View Show notes

Luke is the Art Director of Boat Magazine, a magazine that travels to, and reports from, a different city each issue. We talk about how Luke got into design, what he does in his day job and how he manages to balance that with his work on a nomadic magazine.

About Mag Heroes

Mag Heroes is a new audio show that talks with people who make magazines.

Since launching Magpile I've been in contact with a great group of publishers, editors and designers. I've come to realise that many of these people are hidden behind their magazines, which is something I aim to tackle with Mag Heroes, where I find out where they came from, how they got into magazines, and explore their day-to-day processes and inspiration.
